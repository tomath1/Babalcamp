About the camp

Welcome to Babel Camp, where adventure meets comfort in the heart of nature. Our camp is designed for travelers who seek unforgettable experiences, breathtaking landscapes, and peaceful moments away from the noise of the city. Enjoy stunning views, fresh air, and a warm atmosphere that makes you feel at home. Experience the magic of sleeping under the stars in our luxury dome tents.

Luxury Dome Tents

Experience comfort in our modern geodesic dome tents with panoramic views of the Red Sea mountains.

Cozy Interiors

Fully furnished with air conditioning, comfortable beds, and all amenities for a perfect stay.

Sunset Views

Witness breathtaking sunsets over the Red Sea while enjoying a campfire with friends and family.

📍 Hurghada, Red Sea, Egypt

Babel Camp is located in the beautiful city of Hurghada, on the shores of the stunning Red Sea. Known for its crystal-clear turquoise waters, vibrant coral reefs, and golden sandy beaches, Hurghada is one of Egypt's premier tourist destinations. Our camp offers a unique blend of desert adventure and seaside tranquility, giving you the best of both worlds.
